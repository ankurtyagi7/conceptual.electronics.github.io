About the KI-CAD series

Conceptual Electronics presents: An introduction to KiCad with Ankur Tyagi. In this series Ankur discusses how PCBs are made and the benefits of utilizing KiCad as a design tool and making pro. component layouts. KiCad is a free and open source platform which makes it great for learning how to make your own PCBs and circuit schematic while still being powerful enough to do more complicated design work.
