Electronic prototypes

An electronic prototype is a combination of hardware and software design with custom printed circuit boards (PCB) that are assembled into a fully functional prototype.
Once a concept is developed our first step is to create an Electronic Specification Document that defines the product functionality, componets, pcb sizes and layout within the product casing. We then work with our developers to ensure that the mass production cost is appropriate before completing the development of the electronic hardware. We have the capability to create electronic product prototypes from simple gadgets through to complex machines and systems with many components.
